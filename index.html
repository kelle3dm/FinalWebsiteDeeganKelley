Purpose

This website is for anyone struggling with their mental and physical health during COVID-19. It is built to help people overcome the uncertainties and motivating people into a healthier lifestyle even while staying at home.

What We Do

We are here to remind people they are not alone in this pandemic. It is extremely easy to feel isolated during these times and we have come together to present a way to bring joy and comfort in the craziness of COVID-19. We are here for you, you are never alone. To learn more about Never Alone, click here.

Sign Up

If you are intersted in receiving our newsletter click here to fill out a form so we can send you fun things to do, workouts, recipies, positive vibes, and coping strategies! The best part about this newsletter is that it is tailored to what you want to see and how often you want to recieve notifications from us.

Get the Help You Need

Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you are having any suicidal thoughts or are struggling to find a reason to live please call 800-273-8255, call 9-1-1, or click here.I hope you know that you are so loved, worthy, and valued and your life is precious-- we are here to help you get the help you need during this hard time!
