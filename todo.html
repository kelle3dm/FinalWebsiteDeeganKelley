Declutter

Go through your house and find things that you do not need and donate them to a local thrift store drop box or homeless shelter. It will make you feel less stressed, cluttered, and you will be donating to a good cause!

Even though it might be chilly out, bundle up, wear your mask, and get some fresh air. Walking is very good for both your physical and mental health!
