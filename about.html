Our Mission

We want you to know that you are never alone in this global pandemic. This time has been hard on all of us whether we like to admit it or not. We want our viewers to feel like they have somewhere positive instead of sitting in anxious or depressed thoughts. Our mission is rooted on wanting to make everyone feel worthy, valued, and loved. That there is a purpose, there is growth that can happen, and there is light even admist a global hardship as COVID-19 has been.

About the Founder

My name is Deegan Kelley and from struggling myself with my mental health during this pandemic, I decided to make this online community. I want to encourage people during this time and to let them know they are never alone in this physical and mental health deprivation caused by COVID-19. I hope that you are able to find peace during this time and joy in the little things.




Joshua 1:9

This organization was based off of the Bible verse Joshua 1:9,"Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go." This verse is very encouraging because it tells us that the Lord is always with us, he will never leave us, he has a plan during this time, and he will comfort us during the hardships and trials of COVID-19. My hopes are that you would receieve the Lord and his love for you.
